Our Process

Since we care about client experience and quality products, we've created a streamlined process for our clients. In this process, you will meet Brittany, get a consultation and session time, and an in person presentation of your pictures. We've setup 3 easy to follow steps:

1

An initial meeting will be scheduled between Brittany and any potential clients for a general consultation.

2

A session time will be reserved and the session fee will be collected.

3

An in-person reveal session where our clients can view the photos and select their products.
